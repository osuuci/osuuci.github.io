About

osu! UCI is a recently founded osu! club at the University of California, Irvine. We focus around playing and discussing osu!, a free, frustrating, but fun rhythm game. Though based in Irvine, any fellow osu! player is free to hangout with us online and offline. If interested, join us in our Facebook group where we handle most our organization. Keep on the lookout for our weekly events!
