About

osu! UCI was founded in 2014 by Max Chung at the University of California, Irvine. We focus around playing and discussing osu!, a free, frustrating, but fun rhythm game. Though based in Irvine, any fellow osu! player is free to hangout with us online and offline. If interested, join us in our Discord where we handle most of our organization. Be on the lookout for our weekly events!
