About

osu! UCI is a recently founded osu! club at the University of California, Irvine. We focus around playing and discussing osu!, a free, frustrating, but fun rhythm game. Though based in Irvine, any fellow osu! player is free to hangout with us online and offline. If interested, join us in our Facebook group where we handle most our organization. Keep on the lookout for our weekly events!

Meet the cult!

Meetings

Our in-person meetings for this fall quarter will be at 6PM on Fridays. Find us at Engineering Tower 201 (ET 201). It's a room on the outside perimeter of the building, so just circle around the sides until you find it.

We will also be hosting online lobbies each weekend. These will be on Saturdays starting at 7PM PDT/GMT-7 and ending around 10PM PDT/GMT-7.
