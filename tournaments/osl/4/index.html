Schedule

We ran into some problems with resetting the Challonge brackets, so we'll be releasing the schedule a day late. They should be available Tuesday night to early Wednesday PDT.

Please email us at osuuci@gmail.com if you have any conflicts with your match. You can message a tournament admin, but mail is preferred so that all the admins can see the same message, and there'll be less mistakes and overhead as we try and coordinate together. And if you want to help us out and save back-and-forth in planning a reschedule, reach out to your opponent first and agree to a time before finalizing the change with us.

  • Load your current beatmaps by pointing to your root osu! folder
  • Open our collection file
  • Search for and download beatmaps that you are missing
  • Join our collection file with your collection file
  • Save the joined collection file as your new osu! collections
