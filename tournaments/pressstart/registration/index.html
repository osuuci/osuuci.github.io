Housing

Press Start will not be officially sanctioning any sleeping areas in the event. You are allowed to "rest" at your desk, but sleeping overnight is not allowed. For those of you staying overnight between the 9-10th, we realize that going back and forth between hotels can be costly, inefficient, and far away, so in response, Jimmy and I have talked about starting a makeshift hostel system to help with housing.

This will involve placing groups of you within our own apartment/dorm quarters for the night. Nothing fancy, just somewhere to lay down for the night and go back to the event in the morning. Please don't make a mess for us, and please bring your own sleeping materials and toiletries. In total, we can fit probably close to a dozen comfortably, and if we push it, maybe a few more than that. All the officers will try and spare some space for housing.

As numbers and organization is a concern, we will only be offering this housing option for any entrants for the osu! tournament. Additionally, we will ask for $10 as payment. This is to act as security and responsibility on your part as well as relieving some of the upkeep costs of the club and our time spent in organizing this tournament. We feel this is a pretty reasonable offer, and if you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to us.
