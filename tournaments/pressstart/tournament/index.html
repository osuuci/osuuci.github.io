Tournament Structure

  • Refer to the Brackets page for the tournament bracket information
  • 2 Swiss groups will be made based on the rank seeding of players; see an example of one Swiss group:

  • Each Swiss group will play 4 rounds
  • The top 4 of each Swiss group advances to a single elimination bracket; see our example bracket:

  • The top 8 bracket will also be streamed on Twitch
  • Bo3 Swiss groups, Bo5 top 8 quarter finals, Bo7 semifinals, Bo9 grand finals

Rules

  • Players must be 13 years or older to enter
  • Players under 18 must have parents/guardians sign a waiver located on site
  • Player accounts must be in good standing
  • Players must be physically present within the LAN to play
  • Matches are in osu! standard with score as the win condition
  • !roll begins every match, higher roll wins
  • Roll loser decides warm up map (any song <3 minutes, not in map pool)
  • Roll winner decides first map
  • Thereafter, loser decides next map
  • No map bans
  • In the top 8, each mod can only be selected once by a respective player
  • Tournament rules and structure are subject to alterations by the administrators; administrators have the final say in any disputes
  • New players may sign up during the setup period provided that spots still remain or become vacant
  • Missing a time slot (registration, matches, etc.) counts as a loss; please show up a few minutes before your alotted time slot so you and the administrators can set up in time

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, contact an osu! UCI officer. Jimmy and I(Max) are the main ones in charge of the tournament, but any of the officers will be happy to help out. We'll be manning the osu! booth the day of to handle administration and questions. In addition to contacting us through osu!, email, or in person, also make sure to follow our Twitter updates of the event, as we may be tweeting our important announcements, delays, changes, etc.
